Welcome to Karam El-Sham

Karam El-Sham restaurants have been operating in Egypt for approximately 4 to 5 years, quickly gaining widespread popularity for their high-quality food and authentic Syrian flavors.

The chain offers a rich variety of Levantine dishes, including shawarma, fatta, grilled meats, and renowned Syrian appetizers like tabbouleh and fattoush.
